# Product Portfolio

The Conso4s product portfolio is focused on protecting and empowering what matters.

Conso4s builds practical software products across AI, security, productivity, governance and business automation.

This portfolio is not a collection of demos. Each product exists because we found a real operating problem that needed a working system, not another slide deck.

## Protect What Matters

### VeriSense

Protecting humans from scams, fraud and digital manipulation.

VeriSense helps people check suspicious messages, images, links, requests and digital interactions before they trust, pay, click or reply.

Website: https://verisense.app

---

### Kayvrn

External security exposure scanning.

Kayvrn helps organisations understand what is publicly visible, exposed, misconfigured or risky from the outside.

The focus is evidence-led external visibility without exploit-based testing by default.

Website: https://kayvrn.com

---

### TrustedPods

Private invite-only social spaces for families and trusted groups.

TrustedPods is built around a simple belief: not every social product needs discovery. Some spaces should be private, small, trusted and intentional.

Website: https://trustedpods.app

Status: coming soon

## Empower What Matters

### Appfora

Product launch essentials for developers.

Appfora helps developers turn an app into a market-ready product by supporting the product layer around the code: legal page drafts, support content, growth material, test journeys and documentation.

Website: https://appfora.io

---

### WeeklyReport

Governance, auditability and confidentiality in reporting.

WeeklyReport is built around the belief that project truth should not live in a slide deck. Reports should expose blockers, risks, decisions and ownership before problems become escalation.

Website: https://weeklyreport.io

---

### TaskSpread

Uncluttered workload management.

TaskSpread focuses on work that actually moves, reducing the noise and overhead that often builds up inside task and workload tools.

Website: https://taskspread.app

---

### HeyPostly

AI-generated hashtag and social posting support.

HeyPostly helps small teams and founders improve social posting without turning content planning into a full-time job.

Website: https://heypostly.com

---

### ProdExplainer

Explainer, onboarding, training videos and social post generation.

ProdExplainer helps products explain themselves through practical video and content assets for onboarding, training, demos and social channels.

Website: https://prodexplainer.com

Status: coming soon

---

### EntMaz

Autonomous self-configuring ERP.

EntMaz is being built around the idea that ERP should configure around the business instead of creating months of consultant dependency.

Website: https://entmaz.com
